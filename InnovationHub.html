Process

Every build is a collaboration between researchers and designers. We focus on solutions to the daily problems researchers face, not on designing a product or thing for its own sake. The hub is structured to keep ideas moving and costs lean.

01 - Listen

Map the task flow, pin down pain points, and align on success metrics.

02 - Shape

Design for usability, print speed, and durability with rapid feedback loops.

03 - Print

Prototype with tuned FDM materials and refine for sterilization needs.

04 - Deliver

Ship or share files, plus documentation and assembly guidance.
